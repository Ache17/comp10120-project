Our Introduction
 We are students at the University of Manchester in the UK and we created SongSpace

This is a website that helps you manage and display your music list

You can use SongSpace to fully manage and enjoy music.

SongSpace provides an alternative means of creating, viewing, and sorting your actual playlists. You can post your custom to make playlists to a public space, which can then be reviewed by other users on the site.
